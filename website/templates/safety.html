BE PREPARED!

First of all, you have to be prepared for a wildfire.
How can you do that?
ensure that you have an emergency plan
stay informed of weather conditions and advisories in your area
be aware of potentially triggering weather conditions, such as droughts and lightning storms

During a wildfire:
keep all doors and windows closed in your home.
keep lights on to aid visibility in case smoke fills the house.
be aware of any downed power lines which can cause electrocution; be sure to stay at least 10 metres away from them to avoid injury

How to prepare your home?
remove any fire hazards in and around your home, such as dried out branches, leaves and debris
test your smoke detectors once per month
consult with your local fire department or a qualified engineer about making your home fire-resistant
